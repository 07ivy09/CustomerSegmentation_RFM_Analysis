## ğŸ§® RFM Analysis Methodology

### Recency (R)
- Measures how recently a customer made a purchase  
- Lower recency indicates higher engagement

### Frequency (F)
- Measures how often a customer makes purchases  
- Higher frequency indicates loyalty

### Monetary (M)
- Measures total revenue generated by a customer  
- Higher monetary value indicates higher business impact

Customers are scored and grouped into actionable segments using business-defined thresholds.
